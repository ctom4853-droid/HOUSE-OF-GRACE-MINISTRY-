About Us

Welcome to House of Grace

House of Grace Church is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Under the leadership of Apostle Wilson Odiase, we are committed to teaching the undiluted Word of God and raising a generation of truth-seekers.

Our philosophy is rooted in the truth that "truth will not always be fare to those that are against the truth, but we can do nothing against the truth, but for the truth" (2 Corinthians 13:8).

"Truth faces many confrontations. They call it rebel, stubborn, proud, self acclaimed righteous, or in this side of the world, they call it 'I too know'"

— Apostle Wilson Odiase
